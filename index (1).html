Our Story

An alliance of scent
and kindle

Scent and Kindle began in a small workshop in Geoffroy, Bambous — a corner of Mauritius where the air already smells of vanilla and salt. What started as candles poured for family became a full maison of fragrance: florals, ambers, musks and woods, each named for the feeling it was made to hold.

Every bougie is still hand-poured in small batches, tested by scent before it's tested by anything else, and finished by hand — from the first pour to the label on the jar.
